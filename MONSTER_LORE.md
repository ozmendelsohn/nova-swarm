# Monster Lore & Quirks

One entry per knot of the Unraveling: its fiction and its unique behavior
(implemented in `js/enemies.js`, `MQUIRKS`). Grown batch by batch.

## Batch 1 — the first knots

| Monster | Lore | Quirk |
|---|---|---|
| Slime | Spilled dye that learned to want. | **Pooling** — splits into two droplets on death |
| Bat | A scrap of night the Weaver trimmed off and never swept up. | **Skittish** — flutters in erratic arcs, hard to lead |
| Beetle | It carries a tile of the old pattern on its back and lets nothing touch it. | **Shellback** — periodically hunkers: near-motionless but takes half damage |
| Shroom | It grew in the dark between two stitches and thinks the dark is coming back. | **Spore Burst** — death releases a ring of slow toxin |
| Eyeball | The Loom needed to see what was eating it. It regrets looking. | **Fixation** — lunges at you every few seconds |
| Imp | A knot tied as a joke. The joke kept going. | **Mischief** — sometimes blinks away when struck |
| Crab | It walks sideways because the weft runs that way. Obviously. | **Sidestep** — approaches in strafing arcs |
| Spider | The only knot that learned to weave back. It weaves badly, on purpose. | **Silkspinner** — leaves web patches that slow YOU |
| Jelly | Dye and water and a rumor of a soul. | **Stinging Current** — pops into slow drifting stingers |
| Skull Mage | It remembers being embroidery on a funeral shroud. It misses the work. | **Warding** — shields a nearby knot for half damage |
| Hornet | The Weaver kept bees once. These are not those. | **Committed Dive** — locks a line and charges down it |
| Wraith | Weft with no warp: a direction with nothing to hold it. | **Phasewalk** — periodically untouchable (visibly faded) |
| Serpent | One long thread that refused to be cut to length. | **Sidewinder** — weaves in S-curves |

*(Remaining 26 monsters + 6 bosses follow in subsequent passes.)*
